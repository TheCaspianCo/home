Last Updated: December 27, 2025

2. How Affiliate Links Work

When you click on a product link on our website:

  • The commission comes from the retailer, not from you
  • You pay the same price as you would if you visited the site directly
  • There are no hidden fees or additional charges

4. Your Purchase Experience

Important things to know:

  • We are not the seller of any products
